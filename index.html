Our Story

A Family Built on the Sea

Hook, Spear & Fire Tours was born from a lifetime on the water in San Pedro, Ambergris Caye. What started as a father sharing his love of the reef with his children has grown into a full-service tour operation — but the soul of the company hasn't changed one bit.

We know every coral head, every current, every secret channel between here and the outer atolls. Our guides aren't just experienced — they grew up here. This reef is their backyard, and they can't wait to show it to you.

Whether you're a first-time snorkeler or an experienced free-dive spearfisherman, we'll craft an experience that fits you perfectly. Small groups, private charters, family trips — we do it all.
